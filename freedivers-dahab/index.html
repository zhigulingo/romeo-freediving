About

Romeo — Molchanovs W2I, PB 58m

I'm Romeo — a Molchanovs Wave 2 Instructor, AIDA & CMAS athlete with a personal best of 58 m. Based in Dahab, I shoot freedivers on their training dives — Blue Hole, Lighthouse, Bells.

I shoot full-frame Sony A7C for portfolio-grade frames and DJI Osmo Action 6 for POV and Reels-ready dynamics. The shoot doesn't break your training session — I move with you, time the apnea, and frame the line.

Because I'm a freediver too, I know what kind of frames make a 60 m mouthfill or a clean turn actually look like depth. That's the difference between a tourist photo and a freediver's photo.
