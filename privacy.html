Privacy & Cookie Policy

This page explains what data we collect on romeofreediving.com and why. Romeo Freediving is an individual photographer based in Dahab, Egypt. By using this site you agree to the practices described below.

What we collect

We do not run user accounts and do not collect names or contact details directly through this site. The site uses cookies and similar technologies to measure traffic and ad performance. This includes anonymous identifiers, browser and device info, approximate location, pages viewed, and referring source.

Why we use it

To understand which pages and ads work, to improve the site, and to show relevant ads on third-party platforms. We do not sell personal data.

Third-party services

  • Yandex Metrica — analytics and visit recording.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook / Instagram) — ad attribution and audience building.
  • Google Fonts & CDN — typography and script delivery.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and may transfer data outside your country. You can opt out via your browser settings or platform-specific tools.

Your choices

You can clear cookies in your browser at any time, use private/incognito mode, or install a tracker-blocking extension. Mobile users can reset advertising IDs in system settings.

Contact

Questions or removal requests — message us on Telegram (link in the site menu) or write to the contact address listed there.
